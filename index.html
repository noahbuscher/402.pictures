Studio 402 is an affordable space for creatives in Lincoln to create and showcase their work. But we're more than just a space... we're a community.

We are focused on providing an accessible location for beginners and experienced creators alike to inhabit.

Let's be real: one of the hardest things about being a creative in the Midwest is a lack of resources. We're here to bridge that gap by providing mutiple studios, including photo studios, a recording studio, and a showroom to rent and make amazing things in.

In addition to providing the locations, we also supplement with equiptment such as lighting, backdrops, keyboards, mixers, and more to get your project off the ground! And every space doubles as a meeting room to sit down with clients.

Rates —

We get it that independent creators have it hard: you're an artist, manage your own business, and often even end up being a taxi driver for you clients. You can rest easy knowing we're extremely affordable in additon to being completely transparent.

Per Hour

$20/hr

Need a studio space to snap a few portraits, record a new track, or meet with your client? We got your back. Just block off the time in the studio and submit payment online! It's really that easy.

Monthly

$75/month

Think you're gonna need the spaces quite a bit? Snag a monthly membership to get access to any of our four studios from 9-5! Just be sure to block off the time you're using it for. Cancel anytime. (maximum of 20 hours)
