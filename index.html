Studio 402 is an affordable space for creatives in Lincoln to take photos and meet with clients. But we're more than just a space... we're a community.

We are focused on providing an accessible studio for beginners and photography experts alike to inhabit.

Let's be real: one of the hardest things about being a photographer in the Midwest is the supossed lack of photo locations, expecially if you don't have access to a studio! That's where we come in... for $20/hour you can have your own studio space and meeting room that you can use to take portraits, artisitc shoots, do product photography, and get crazy!

Studio 402 is owned by two Lincoln photographers, Noah Buscher and Amanda Leise, and we know what photographers need to have a sucessful shoot. That's why we provide studio lighting, backdrops, props, equiptment, and, of course, coffee! So what are you waiting for?

Rates.
