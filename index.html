I am a developer and photographer based out of Lincoln, Nebraska. I try to do things unique, not adhering to conventional standards of beauty or form.

Each project I take on is a new challenge, a new frontier to be conquered. I don't ship something unless I am proud to put my name behind it.

When I start working on a project with someone, the are not viewed by me as a client, but instead as a friend. Want to know how a site is coming along? Just text me. I believe in delivering experiences, and if you need something changed even months or years down the line, don't hesitate to contact me. I got your back.

I stick to my Midwest roots and have a no-bullshit policy. With me, what ya see is what ya get. I deliver honestly and tell you the skinny upfront. Let's work together.
